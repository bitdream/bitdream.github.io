Easy Crossword with More Clues is a crossword puzzle game designed for beginners, or English as second language (ESL) learner. Compared with classic crosswords, one innovation is it has 5x clues for each word which makes the crosswords more friendly to the beginners. You can see more clues by touching the clue description label.

If you are new to crosswords or have a vocabulary less than 10000 and yet you still want to challenge yourself with crossword, it will be your best friend. It will be a great tool if you want to enlarge your English vocabulary and practice them.

How to proceed when you meet obstacle?

  • 1 Touch the clue/description label for the word and you will see the next clue. There are usually 5 clues for each word. In the left bottom there is a menu button, there are options to get help.
  • 2 Reveal relevant. Only corresponding letters of the word will be left.
  • 3 Reveal word.
  • 4 Reveal puzzle.
  • 5 Ask friends with a text message or mail.
  • 6 Ask friends with an image.
How to earn more stars?

  • 1 Solve more puzzles. Every single solved word will give you stars.
  • 2 Watch rewarded AD.
  • 3 Upgrades to pro version.

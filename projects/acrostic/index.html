Acrostic: Trivia Crossword

Solve acrostic trivia crossword puzzles and discover interesting trivia facts.

An acrostic is a type of word puzzle, related somewhat to crossword puzzles, that uses an acrostic form. It consists of two parts. The first part is a set of lettered clues, each of which has numbered blanks representing the letters of the answer. The second part is a long series of numbered blanks and spaces, representing a quotation or other text and trivia facts in this game, into which the answers for the clues fit. In some forms of the puzzle, the first letters of each correct clue answer, read in order from clue A on down the list, will spell out the author of the quote and the title of the work it is taken from; this can be used as an additional solving aid.
